Words Matter

I've always loved writing. Maybe it's because I've always been a rule follower or because I'm a perfectionist. Regardless, I believe words matter. The words we choose to communicate, whether written or electronic, are so important. They not only set the tone for a conversation, they also help or hinder the understanding of your audience. Over the past 20 years, I've primarily worked in Learning & Development. From creating synchronous and asynchronous training content to procedures, emails, newsletters, FAQs, video scripts, and websites, I've had amazing opportunities to create it all.
